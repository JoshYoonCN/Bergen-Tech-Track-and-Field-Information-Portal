The Bergen County Technical High School District is a member of the Big North Conference Liberty Division. The Knights Track and Field team represent students from all four of our campuses, Hackensack, Teterboro, Paramus and Applied Technology. Track and Field is played at the Varsity, Junior Varsity, and Freshman levels. The district provides transportation from school to practices and from practices to a central drop-off location in the athlete's home district.
